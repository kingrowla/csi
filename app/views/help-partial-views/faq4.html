How to Change Cache Setting in Internet Explorer

1. On the Menu Bar, click on Tools, then select Internet Options.

2. Under the General Tab, click on the Settings button in the Browsing History Section.

3. Select "Every Time I Visit The Webpage" radio button, click OK button. Also select Delete... under the Browsing history section to clear out your temporary internet files (cache).

4. Click OK button again.

Note: If this feature is disabled, then you will need to contact your network administrator.
