How to Create a Link to Your Desktop

Step One:
Right click on your desktop, choose NEW, then SHORTCUT

Step Two:

On the text box, copy the url that was emailed to you. (If you need the url, please email request to help@collectorsolutions.com) Paste the url into the text box (shown below), and click on NEXT


Step Three:

Type the name (i.e. CSI Management Console )you want to give it in the text box. (see below), then click on FINISH.


An icon is created, and placed on your desktop. Now, click on that icon to open the CollectorSolutions Management Console.
